The Cosmere

The Cosmere is a universe made by Brandon Sanderson which is the setting for not only the world of Roshar, the world of The Stormlight Archive, but also many others. He has create an interconnected series of book series. These book series each have their own lore and magic systems but share an overarching lore and their magic systems share fundamental components. You do not have to read every book in the cosmere to understand or enjoy books fromn other series in the Cosmere, however, reading as many books from the cosmere will enhance the experience as you will be able see connections and understand how things work in the overall cosmere.
